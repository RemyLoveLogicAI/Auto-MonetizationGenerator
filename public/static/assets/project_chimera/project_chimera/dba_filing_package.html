Step-by-Step Filing Instructions

1

Retrieve LoveLogic AI LLC Documents

Log into your LegalZoom account at www.legalzoom.com/my/login

Navigate to Documents → eSignature → All files to download your Articles of Organization

2

Complete FBN Application Online

Visit Contra Costa County FBN Portal

Fill out the form online, print, sign, and mail with payment

3

Arrange Newspaper Publication

Contact one of the approved newspapers listed below

Publication must run for 4 consecutive weeks

4

Submit Forms and Payment

Mail completed forms with check/money order to Contra Costa County Clerk

Include copy of LoveLogic AI LLC Articles of Organization

5

File Proof of Publication

After publication is complete, file the affidavit of publication with the county

This completes your DBA filing process
